GDPR Policy:

As you may know, the GDPR comes into force on 25 May 2018 and will change how all organisations handle personal data.
Oyster Property Surveyors Ltd will be classified as the Data Controller for this data, we are required to gain your consent to hold your data for us to continue sending you emails regarding improvements to our services.
The only personal data we collect from you is your name, contact telephone number, address and email address.
This data is stored electronically in a folder on our server.
Your data will be used to send you emails regarding improvements to our services.
All emails are sent “blind-copied” so no other recipients will get hold of your email address.
No data is transferred to any third parties.
We may use third-party Service Providers to monitor and analyze the use of our Service.
