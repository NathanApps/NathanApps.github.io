About Us

Welcome to Oyster Property Surveyors Ltd

We are local and independent RICS regulated Building Surveying practice based in Whitstable with over 25 years surveying experience and extensive knowledge of Kent.


Oyster Property Surveyors Ltd was founded in 2018 by its principal surveyor Richard Glanville and is a RICS regulated firm.

With over 25 years of experience in property surveying including project and contract management to most archetypes of buildings including Grade I and II listed in both private and public sectors, working throughout East and West Kent.

We specialise in surveying residential and commercial properties for RICS homebuyer reports, property condition surveys, full building surveys, commercial and new build snagging surveys.

We cover Whitstable, Herne Bay, Beltinge, Faversham, Selling, Canterbury, Bridge, Birchington, Westgate on Sea, Margate, Broadstairs, Ramsgate , Folkestone, Deal, Ashford , Maidstone , Rochester , Chatham and Tunbridge Wells.

Our Mission:

We aim to provide a comprehensive high quality RICS Building Surveying service at competitive price and offer advice you can trust for our clients.

We pride ourselves in excellent customer service.


What We Do:

Residential:

Building surveys and reports, defect investigations and analysis, condition surveys and schedules of condition, RICS homebuyer surveys and reports up to level 3.
The Practice is regulated by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors and are licenced providers of RICS Homebuyer reports.

Commercial:

Bespoke commercial building surveys, acquisition reports, condition surveys and schedules of condition.

Heritage:

Condition / Building Survey Reports. Maintenance schedules and costings.
